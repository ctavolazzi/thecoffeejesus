# The Coffee Jesus - Personal Art and Music Sales Website

Welcome to the official repository for **The Coffee Jesus** - a unique platform where you can sell your art and music directly to your fans and followers. This website is designed to be an evergreen platform that leverages the power of Stripe for seamless transactions.

## Project Overview

**TheCoffeeJesus.com** is intended to be a central hub where I, Christopher Tavolazzi, can showcase and sell my artistic creations and musical compositions. The site aims to provide an easy and engaging experience for fans and followers to purchase and support my work.

## Features

- **Showcase Art and Music**: Display a gallery of my artworks and a library of my musical compositions.
- **Seamless Purchases**: Integrated with Stripe for secure and straightforward payment processing.
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for viewing on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Easy navigation and user-friendly purchase process.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: SvelteKit
- **Backend**: Supabase
- **Payment Processing**: Stripe
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Hosting**: Cloudflare
